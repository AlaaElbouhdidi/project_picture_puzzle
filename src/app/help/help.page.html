Rebus

Rebus is a picture puzzle app.

How to play

Click on the Start button in the home menu and select the module you wanna play.

No module that sparks your interest? Click on the + button to import new modules.

When you have selected a module, click on the puzzle you wanna play.

Your goal is to guess as many pictures as possible.

Have Fun!
